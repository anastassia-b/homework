blini with creme fraiche, eggs, and tarragon


ingredients

  • eggs
  • salmon roe
  • creme fraiche
  • scallions
  • tarragon
  • blini
  • chives
  • blini

directions

  1. make blini.
  2. preheat overn to 425F.
  3. gently fold in chosen ingredients.
  4. pile into nest shapes.
  5. bake for 5 minutes.
